CALCULATION OF BMI PROCEDURE:
These simple steps will help you work out your BMI:
1. Multiply your height in meters(m) by itself
2. Divide your weight in kilograms (kg) by your step 1

FORMULA(you can calculate by yourself):
BMI = x/(y*y)
Where x=bodyweight in KG, y=height in cm
